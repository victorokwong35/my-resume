About Me

My name is Victor Itah. I'm a young Front End Engineer and designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I describe myself as a passionate designer and developer who loves coding, open source, designing and the web platform.

Aside from my job, I like to create and contribute to open source projects. This helps me to learn lots of stuff, grow as a designer, developer and support other open source projects. Also I enjoy doing Ui/Ux personally because it give room for foundational layout, guidelines as well as creativity exercise and approach before the actual coding.

Throughout my career, I have always tried to ensure that the passion and enthusiasm for my work has shone through. I have found that it is through a team that I have achieved my greatest potential. In all my work, I have relished these challenges and used them to fuel my ambition and maintain my own high standards.

In my free time you can find me gaming, studying or on tech meetups and conference
