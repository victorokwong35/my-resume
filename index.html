About Me

My name is Victor Itah. I'm a Front End Developer, Ui-Ux Designer and a Creative Designer. I describe myself as a passionate designer and developer who loves designing and developing projects.

As an experienced developer, my main library for development is ReactJs and often times i also make use of the VueJs library. Aside from my job, I like to create and contribute to open-source projects. This helps me to learn alot while growing as a developer and support broader open-source projects as well. I am also a creative and experienced Ui/Ux designer that loves designing and prototyping. I believe that doing this gives room for foundational architecture and design of an application before proceeding to the development phase.

Throughout my career, I have always tried to ensure that the passion and enthusiasm for my work have shone through. I have found that it is through a team that I have achieved my greatest potential.
In all my work, I have relished these challenges and used them to fuel my ambition and maintain my own standards.
